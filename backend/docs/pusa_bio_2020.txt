The PUSA bio-decomposer is a microbial solution developed by IARI that decomposes paddy stubble in 15-20 days. Application: Mix 4 capsules in 25 liters of jaggery solution, ferment for 5 days, then dilute to 500L and spray per acre. Cost: ₹20/acre vs ₹5,000-6,000/acre for mechanical management. Reduces CH₄ and N₂O emissions by 30-40%.
